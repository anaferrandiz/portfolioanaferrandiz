Moussa shares some reflections on the decisions and aspirations throughout his life. He was born in Mbour (Senegal), and after learning about sewing (among many other things) there, he decided to start his business in Valencia (Spain). He creates and customizes all kinds of clothing with fabrics from Senegal. Additionally, he is also a dance teacher. He shares his culture through everything he does and aspires to continue creating and learning from everything around him.
